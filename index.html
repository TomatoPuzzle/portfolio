About Me

I'm Miraj, a dedicated Data Analyst and BI Engineer with a passion for turning complex data into clear, actionable insights. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Technology Malaysia, where I earned Dean's List recognition multiple times.

With certifications as a Power BI Data Analyst Associate (PL-300), Azure Data Scientist Associate (DP-100), and Fabric Analytics Engineer Associate (DP-600), I am proficient in Excel, Python, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI. My academic journey, including studying abroad, has strengthened my adaptability and problem-solving skills across diverse cultural settings.

Notably, I earned an A+ for my final year project, the "Save Me Accident Alert & Notification System," an innovative embedded system designed to rapidly alert and send notification to users about accident locations.
