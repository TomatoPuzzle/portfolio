Customer Targeting Analysis

Advising a client on customer targeting strategies by analyzing demographic and transactional data to identify high-value segments.

Situation

During a simulation at KPMG AU, I was tasked with advising a client on customer targeting. This involved assessing data quality, identifying high-value customers, and presenting findings through interactive dashboards.

Interactive Tableau Dashboard

Task

The primary responsibilities included:

  • Data Quality Assessment: Evaluating datasets for completeness and reliability.
  • Target Analysis: Identifying high-value customers based on demographics and attributes.
  • Dashboard Development: Creating visual reports to effectively communicate insights.

Action

To address these tasks, I scrutinized the data for accuracy, analyzed demographic trends to segment high-value customers, and designed a Tableau dashboard to present these insights clearly.

Dashboard Highlights & Results

💰 Product Line Costs

Analyzed Standard, Road, Touring, and Mountain costs vs. List Prices to determine profitability margins.

👥 Demographics

Provided a detailed breakdown of customer distribution by age and gender to tailor marketing efforts.

🛒 Transaction Channels

Compared Online vs. Offline transactions across states to reveal regional purchasing preferences.

🏷️ Brand Insights

Identified top-performing brands and product lines based on transaction volume.

Conclusion

The dashboards successfully communicated complex findings in a visually compelling manner, enabling the client to make informed decisions about targeting high-value customers. The analysis provided valuable insights into product costs, demographic distribution, and transaction preferences, enhancing the client's ability to tailor their strategies effectively.
